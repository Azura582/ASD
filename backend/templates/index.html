Usage Instructions

About the Survey

The survey is based on standardized ASD screening questionnaires. By observing daily behaviors and social interactions it estimates the level of ASD risk. Suitable for children approximately 18 months to 4 years old.

About Image Detection

Image detection uses deep learning models to identify typical ASD-related behaviors such as head_banging, spinning, and hand_flapping.

Important: This system is for screening reference only and does not replace professional medical diagnosis. If you have concerns, please consult a qualified clinician.
